Terms & Conditions

By commissioning me, you agree to the terms listed below.

General

  • All artwork is digital only unless otherwise arranged.
  • Final artwork is for personal use only unless commercial rights are purchased separately.
  • I reserve the right to decline any commission for any reason.
  • Clients must provide clear visual references; I may decline heavily text-only descriptions.

Payment

  • Payment is accepted in USD via PayPal or Venmo.
  • Full payment upfront is required before I begin working on the piece.
  • Chargebacks are not allowed. Please contact me directly if an issue arises.

Process & Revisions

  • I will provide a sketch or WIP for approval unless the chosen style does not require one.
  • One round of minor revisions (small details, color tweaks) is included for free.
  • Major changes (pose, outfit, composition) requested after sketch approval may incur an additional fee.
  • No revisions are accepted once the final artwork has been delivered.

Timeline

  • Typical turnaround time is 1‚Äì3 weeks, depending on queue and complexity.
  • Delays will always be communicated promptly.

Refund Policy

  • A full refund is available only if I have not started the piece.
  • A partial refund may be granted if the piece is partially completed.
  • No refunds are provided once the artwork is finished and delivered.
  • If I am unable to complete your commission, you will receive a full refund.
